About

Emerging from the outskirts of Columbus, Ohio, Outsider Heart shimmers under the dying light of rock and roll. Combining angsty pop melodies and gritty alt-rock instrumentation, Outsider Heart blends fabulous and youthful attitudes in a glimmering new light. Doug Katula (Vocals), Jonah Young (Bass), and Kovy Arseneau (Vocals and Guitar), all worked and collaborated together over the past 3 years, writing numerous songs alongside the likes of David Bendeth, Howard Benson, Mike Plotnikoff, and Will Carlson. The band has accumulated a plethora of tracks that blur the lines between explorative drive and melodic prowess.
